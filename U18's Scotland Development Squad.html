Rugby- Core Skills

Passing

Passing will be a core part of their role, being able to deliver a variety of passes effectively or selecting the best pass to use in different situations.
Running

Key running skills and multi-directional running is common to all players: acceleration, changing pace, changing direction and running efficiently sideways and backwards.Players need to be balanced when they are running, especially just before contact situations.
Suppport Play

Players need to understand their supporting roles in the game.Coming from a deep position behind the ball carrier and accelerate onto the pass. They must communicate accurately with the ball carrier to tell him exactly where they are and when they want the pass.
Tackling

Players need to be able to execute a variety of tackles in different situations, and to make these tackles efficiently. All players are competent tackling in one-on-one situations.
Decision Making It's important that all your players learn how to make good decisions under pressure the majority of the time. As with most complex skills, decision making can only be developed by making real-time decisions repeatedly.
